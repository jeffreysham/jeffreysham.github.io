Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

My Alexa Skills (Party Bouncer, Kids Madlibs, and Class Schedule) do not share any of your personal information. Party Bouncer uses the information you tell it to determine whether guests are on the list or not. Kids Madlibs uses your input to form a silly story. Class schedule uses the class information you input to tell you what classes you have inputted. It also retrieves your device's time zone in order to determine what classes you have today. The information given to the skills are stored on the server (AWS Lambda) in order to respond to your requests.
